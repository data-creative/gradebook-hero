About Gradebook Hero

Empowering Students with Seamless Grade Management

Gradebook Hero is an innovative platform that integrates seamlessly with Google Sheets to provide students with an interactive and intuitive dashboard. Our mission is to make it easier for students to stay organized and engaged with their academic responsibilities.

With Gradebook Hero, students can:

  • View assignments and their due dates
  • Track their grades and overall performance
  • Access detailed grade breakdowns and feedback
  • Complete weekly check-ins to monitor progress

Our goal is to equip students with all the tools they need to succeed, providing a clear and comprehensive overview of their academic journey.
